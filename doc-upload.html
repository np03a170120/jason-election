Before Getting Started

To start your video kyc process, please make sure you have the following available

  • Make sure your internet connection is good.
  • Make sure your room is well lit and your face is visible.
  • Make sure your front and back camera has good resolution.
  • Ensure that no one else is in the frame and your face is clearly visible.
