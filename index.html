Namaste, I am
Jason Dev Baidya

I was born on 12 Baisakh 2032, a day earlier to Buddha Purnima, in Shanta Bhawan Hospital, Jhamsikhel, Kathmandu. Tracing back my ancestry I am the 16th generation of the Baidya family, formed by King Harisingha Deva, of Tirhut Kingdom of Simarhaun garh, Bara in BS 1381, by educating one of his Prince ²the Science of Ayurveda and decreeing him to serve the human sufferings as a Baidya (physician) and establishing in Bhaktapur.

Thus my roots point to being of Karnat dynasty connected by marriage, to the Malla linage. My ancestors practiced and excelled in the profession of treating the ailing humanity ever since.

My greatgrandfather Ratna Das Baidya, continued the profession but studied the Western science of Medicine, Allopathy and became the first Nepali Doctor in BS 1959. It was continued by my grand father Raghubar and grand uncle Bharat who were both Doctors and served the country. Another grand uncle Hutaram was the first Agriculture engineer in Nepal.

My father Jaya Dev, however, became a Textile engineer and while I graduated in business management and am involved in my own businesses. With the established business behind me I am now venturing into politics to convert my business skills to social service targeted to serving the society. To bring this effort to fruition I am projecting myself as an independent candidate as your representative and seek your support for the forthcoming elections from the zone three of Lalitpur District.

When my grand father passed away in BS 2047 he had a dying wish to see our family tradition of serving the people through health service. To achieve that noble goal, he instructed my father to fulfil it by sale of some property and see that a scholarship is established to assist diligent and deprived candidates to produce a doctor every year. To attain that, Mathuraratna Scholarship Trust was established in collaboration with the Nepal Medical Association in BS 2051 and deserving candidates are being awarded assistance, since BS 2055.

As a result, 32 MBBS and 9 Post Graduates have been produced, as of today while 8 are presently studying and one is being added every year. I am one of the Trustees and one of my aim in life is to see that the good deed gets perpetuity. To fulfill that, as my spouse and I don't have any offspring, we have worked out a scheme to see that our property goes to the Trust, after our demise. In this psychi of social service, it pains me to see the political scenario and its gradual decline in values and glory of the Nation that had been established by blood and sweat of our ancestors, I feel it not only my duty but am confident that I can positively contribute for the upliftment of the country, I have ventured into politics and this electoral race . For me Politics is a part of Social service.

My education started in St. Xaviers school, Jyawalakhel, Lalitpur. My school days were spent in local circumstances with local friends and family. After completion of SLC in BS 2049, my aim for higher studies took me to Ukraine, Lviv. The beauty of the city inspired me to study architecture. After completing my third year I had to abandon it because of my health. It was due to the very low temperature of the place that caused joint pain. My three years of study was wasted. So, I had to seek warmer place that ended up in the Philippines, where I had to opt for Business management. After graduating, I returned home in BS 2057. However, my dream of building a beautiful city as an architect still remained dormant in me.

Meanwhile, my father had retired from 24 years of service with a multinational company. Inspired by his experience with Industries and as a solution to the waste management problem seen during those days, especially due to plastics, he had started a waste plastic recycling industry in Tandi, Chitwan, in BS 2051. The factory was the first such industry not only in Nepal but also in the Indian subcontinent. It used to process 350 metric tonnes of waste plastic and produce sheets and pipes. 135 local villagers were in employment and the once desert like village had turned into an oasis with happy inhabitants. We still have happy memories of the employees and have a bond with them. The industry was pulling on despite difficulties not only government shortcomings but also due to the undercurrent of the ten year armed uprising. I was also involved as local promoter of Thai Petrochemicals for their plastic raw material and also in Yak&Yeti Hotel as associate director for conference sales. The Maoist armed uprising was in the increase and the industry was under pressure not only for financial support but also even sheltering the materials to manufacture pipe and cooker bombs.

. There was a time the staff were pressured to hide 18 sacs of GI sockets, that are the casings of the bombs, for over two months. The times were getting risky not only from the rebels but also from the authorities. Under these tumultuous times it was getting too risky and impossible to go on. As a result we had no choice but to let go of the industry to those who were waiting to take over. We thought it better to let go than to suffer the traumatic experiences in BS 2064 despite losses. My father kept himself busy in his social service activities, while I took up a non-investment option as a representative of the Phya Thai Hospital of Bangkok and at the same time I also came in contact with another big Thai company CP ALL and was appointed as the local representative of the Panyapiwat Institute for Nepal. Meanwhile, I developed my own business, some joint with friends and some investments. With my passion for music, I have developed it as business. I love playing guitar and I buy special pieces and sell them too. In future I'm planning to go into agriculture compatible to the present technology and low labour high yield projects.

About Election

With the thought that - A people’s representative must remain in close and direct contact with his constituency and the citizens – I pledge to fulfil my duties and responsibilities entrusted to me by the people. To that end, I will create an environment and the means for citizens to directly access and communicate about their issues and suggestions via electronic means and website. Adhering to complete transparency, I will upload and make public the fiduciary details of the expenditure of the government funds made available to the MPs. Likewise, upon the completion of my tenure, I will set the precedent of detailing the completed, in progress and incomplete works and initiatives to be handed over to my successors. I strongly believe that this will contribute positively to the region’s development plans in a continuous and sustainable manner.

Agendas

Creation of a ‘Job Portal App’. My secretariat will initiate the development of the job portal with its scope being expanded continually. This portal will be hosted on smart phone application. Service providers will register their service and expertise.

Living up to the name of our constituency and the culture it represents, I will collaborate with the Lalitpur Metropolitan Office to create avenues for showcasing the art and creative talents of the citizens. Uplift the job opportunities for artists, singers, musicians and theatre sector, so that in future, we shall become a national hub for all artisans to showcase their cultural uniqueness, ability, capability and capacity.

The Nation is at the abyss of local productive activity causing a wide chasm between import and export, causing a wide gap in National balance of payment.I will work towards encouraging new startups and helping them to gain access to the government support facilities for SMEs. I will work to develop access to international markets for local products. Likewise, I will prioritize and facilitate for seeking seed investors for new entrepreneurial projects.

It is imperative that citizens have access to healthy and unadulterated food. My focus will be on prioritizing the health and fitness of citizens first. For this, I will work with the local government to initiate Urban Agriculture program. This will also promote local agriculture and create jobs and will ensure pesticide-free and organic food for citizens.

Urban waste management is in the stage of going out of control due to our ignoring the issue and not handling in a sustainable manner, in the past. If it is not given a right track now before it gets too late, we are sure to face serious environmental crisis in near future. It has to be addressed scientifically and implemented with due legislative measures. I believe, I could use my past hard learned experience to assist the challenge.

The schools of the constituency will undergo an intensive upgrading and reforms in the quality of education. I will focus on introducing the result-based system for developing a pilot government school as a replicable model. The model will be replicated in other schools of the region and serve as an example for the whole nation.

The underprivileged children and students will benefit from scholarship programs in collaboration with the private sector engagement programs. By introducing a culture of giving and helping, I will chart a new course for politics where great examples are set for the people and by the people.

The constituency is prone to road accidents due to recent road infrastructure developments and haphazard driving by heavy load freight vehicles. Particularly around the ring road area, I will work towards minimizing accidents by using cutting edge technology and strict driving rules and regulations. I will prioritize the needs of the pedestrians, cyclists, road lights in order to minimize the road accidents.

Strict implementation of "Reckless Endangerment Act" should come into action to prevent further loss of lives of our fellow citizens. Public vehicle driver will need to acquire special license to operate. By regularly studying the accident data implementation strategy will further calibrated.

There is a growing problem between the Community Housing builders and the customers who bought their community housing property. I personally know few people who have paid the entire agreed amount but the builders have not transfered ownership. Many of such housing projects have not delivered promised common facilities. Some of builders have political protection hence it has become difficult to seek justice. I am also one of the victims of such case. Now it is high time such things needs to be stopped addressed suitably and by formulating laws at the legislative level.

It is my duty to conserve the traditions and culture of the various culture, civilization, and we must preserve the unique aspects of our society, which is our identity, for the coming generations. Therefore, I have dedicated a particular focus to my community’s agenda.

Therefore, I have dedicated a particular focus to my community’s agenda. I believe, this kind of initiative will help promote our unique cultures to a global audience, reflecting in the growth in the tourism sector and, ultimately, our country's economy. This is a relatively easily replicable initiative to document and archive the cultural richness of other communities of our diverse nation..

For example, being from Newar community, the overall vision is to include all the clans and religious sects within the Newa: community and showcase its unique yet diverse aspects by producing a Netflix Special titled “Newars of Nepal” for the world to see. Starting from the significance of Nepal Sambat to the numerous festivals representing Newa: communities spread out all over Nepal, like Biska Jatra from Bhaktapur, Gatha MUGA: Kuuchhi: Bhway, Sitthi Nakha, and other unique rituals from Kirtipur, Palpa, and other Newar settlements from western Nepal will be archived and documented. This can be replicated for projecting the rich cultural diversity of Nepal and promote Tourism to greater heights. These digital archives will feature subtitling in world's major languages and serve as a reference for our future generations. I firmly believe that this initiative can be initiated and completed using national funds and talents. This will also create an opportunity for local talents to showcase their work to a world audience. It is a win-win situation. It can also be an educational material.
